About Us

More about our team

Securisys Private Investigations is located in London, United Kingdom and has a team of approximately fifty licensed investigators placed throughout the London Metro region and around the globe.
Over the years we have built a team of the best investigators in the industry. Each investigator has been hand picked and is specialised in their field.
All investigators are fully licensed and have completed above and beyond the required training and certifications to operate within the industry.
The team at Securisys Private Investigations are motivated, and operate with professionalism and integrity.
We take pride in our customer service and treat each client with the upmost respect and professionalism, and are able to tailor make an investigation to suit their specific requirements.
